Neonatal Echo Simulation

∎

Virtual Neonatal Echocardiographic Training Simulator (VNETS) is developed with exclusive emphasis on neonates and young infants. This system has the capability of simulating real-time scanning from all acoustic windows. It offers the capability of performing real time two-dimensional (2D) echocardiography and color flow Doppler, spectral Doppler, and M-mode modalities from specific sector cuts.
